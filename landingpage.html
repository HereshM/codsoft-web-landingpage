IoT, or Internet of Things, is a network of physical objects that can connect and exchange data with other devices and systems over the internet. These objects are also known as "smart objects" and can include household items, industrial tools, vehicles, and appliances. IoT devices are embedded with sensors, software, and network connectivity, and can communicate with each other and with other internet-enabled devices
